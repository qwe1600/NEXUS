Nexus is an open and accessible online encyclopedia, the project is aimed at creating a global knowledge base in which users can freely search, edit and add information. We strive to ensure that the knowledge is not only accurate and relevant, but also presented in a convenient and understandable form.

Nexus Nexus provides free access to articles

on a variety of topics - from science and technology

to art and history. Anyone can

use the resource, regardless of the location

accommodation and level of education. We call

everyone who wants to join the project and help

to make the world more educated and informed.

Each user can contribute,

creating and editing articles. We believe that

collective cooperation leads to the creation of

high-quality and reliable content.

Create articles, edit existing ones,

add links and illustrations.

Together we can makeNexus better!

We plan to add features for

discussing articles so that users can

share opinions, ask questions and clarify

information.

Join Nexus and help us

to create the most complete and reliable

Internet encyclopedia!

Nexus Nexus is developed with ease of use in mind

on any devices - from desktop

computers to smartphones.
